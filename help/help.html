Help Center

If you are experiencing any issues, please check the common problems below or contact us using the form.

Common Issues

  • Issue 1: Unable to login

    • Solution: Ensure your username and password are correct. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

  • Issue 2: Page not loading

    • Solution: Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache.

  • Issue 3: Video playback issues

    • Solution: Ensure you have the latest version of your browser and that your internet connection is stable.
